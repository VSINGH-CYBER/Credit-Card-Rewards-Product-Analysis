# Success Metrics

## Overview

To evaluate the effectiveness of the Estimated Rewards feature, a variety of customer experience, engagement, and operational metrics should be tracked.

These metrics will help determine whether the feature improves transparency, reduces consumer confusion, and increases interaction with rewards programs.

---

## Primary Metrics

### 1. Reduction in Customer Support Inquiries

**Metric:** Number of support tickets related to rewards discrepancies or transaction confusion  

**Goal:** Decrease in support inquiries related to:
- “Why are my rewards missing?”
- “Why did my rewards change?”
- “Why does my transaction amount look different?”

**Success Indicator:**  
A measurable decrease in support tickets represents improved clarity and transparency.

---

### 2. Increase in Rewards Engagement

**Metric:** User interactions with rewards-related UI elements  

Examples:
- More clicks on rewards details
- Views of transaction reward breakdowns

**Goal:** Increase in user engagement with rewards features  

**Success Indicator:**  
Users are more aware of and interested in their rewards.

---

### 3. Customer Trust & Satisfaction

**Metric:** Customer feedback, app ratings, or survey responses  

**Goal:** Improved sentiment around:
- Transparency
- Trust in rewards calculations
- Clarity of transactions

**Success Indicator:**  
Increase in positive reviews and satisfaction scores or fewer complaints related to rewards confusion.

---

## Secondary Metrics

### 4. Accuracy of Estimated Rewards

**Metric:** Difference between estimated rewards and finalized rewards  

**Goal:** Maintain a high accuracy rate (minimal deviation between estimated and final values)

**Success Indicator:**  
Estimated rewards closely align final rewards in most cases, excluding edge cases.

---

### 5. Feature Usage Rate

**Metric:** Percentage of users who view transactions with estimated rewards  

**Goal:** High visibility and usage of the feature  

**Success Indicator:**  
A large majority of users encounter and interact with estimated rewards.

---

### 6. Time to User Awareness

**Metric:** Time between transaction authorization and user visibility of rewards  

**Goal:** Near real-time visibility of estimated rewards  

**Success Indicator:**  
Users see rewards immediately after transaction authorization.

---

## Summary

Success for this feature is defined by improved transparency, reduced customer confusion, and increased engagement with rewards.

By tracking both behavioral and operational metrics, the impact of the feature can be evaluated from both a customer and business perspective.
