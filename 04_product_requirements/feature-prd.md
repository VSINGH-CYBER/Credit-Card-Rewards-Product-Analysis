
# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Feature: Pending Rewards Estimate

## Objective

The objective of this feature is improve the transparency in the credit card rewards program by implementing a feature that **presents a clearly labeled pending estimate of rewards as soon as a transaction is made**.

At this moment, customers only see rewards after a transaction settles, which can leave room fopr many potential issues such as confusion, uncertainty, and lack of trust in when and how rewards are actually calculated. By implmeneting this feature we will be a able to provide the customer with a fully transparent process in which they are able to view estimated rewards associated with all their pending transactions, as soon as the transaction is made. We will still be able to maintain the ability to adjust final rewards after the amount is fully settled.  

## Problem Statement

Customers often expect immediate feedback when interacting financial products. However, most credit card platfroms delay reward delivery until the transaction is fully settled. 

This delay leads to a sense of uncertainty and confusion when customers are unable to immdiately access their anticipated rewards withe the purchase they have made. Additonally, final settled amounts (such as merchant adjustment and tips) can lead to a final rewards calculations that differs from the customers orginal expectation. 

Improving the tranparency during the pending transaction stage will positively reinforce customer's trust in our reward calculation and allows them to have full insight of the rewards process from the time they swipe their card to the time of the transaction settlement. This is will increase customer engagement and reduce the amount of customer complaints in regards to rewards transparency and calculations.
